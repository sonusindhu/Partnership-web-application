Hawaii Online Continuing Education (CE)

The school

At Digital Learning Centers (DLC) / REMI School of Real Estate, we recognize that as a working professional, you are juggling the demands of work with other commitments. Our CE classes are offered online at your convenience. You can access the courses from anywhere you have an Internet connection and at any time. Work from home, work, an Internet cafe, or even the beach. Pause (or stop) and resume the courses at your convenience. If you missed something, you can review any part (or all) of the course. Best of all, the course presentations are professionally narrated sparing you all the distractions of a traditional classroom environment. We also offer the Hawaii Salesperson Course, Hawaii Broker Course, and Hawaii Test Prep Course. If you have questions, simply email Hawaii@DigitalLearningCenters.com. Your email will usually be answered within several hours. Our staff is committed to helping you. Whether you need clarification of course material or technical support. We are here to assist you.

DLC welcomes all applicants regardless of race, color, religion, ancestry / national origin, sex, sexual orientation, familial status, disability, marital status, age, AIDS status or other grounds protected under state or federal non-discrimination laws.

Principal Instructor

David Catanzaro serves as the primary instructor for Digital Learning Centers. In 1970, he earned his Bachelors Degree in Finance and later (in 1973) his Masters Degree in Business Administration (MBA) from the University of Missouri. David has been a real estate agent since 1986. He was a Broker and Instructor with Dower Real Estate, Inc; then the principle broker for Dower Realty Windward, an independently owned and operated franchise of Dower Realty; and is now the principle broker for REMI Realty.

Location

Digital Learning Centers specializes in online learning. Our CE courses are completely online. You can access them from anywhere at your convenience using the Internet.

Our corporate offices are located on the postcard-perfect Windward-side of Oahu -- a 30-minute drive from the hustle and bustle of Honolulu and Waikiki -- near the beautiful and award winning beaches of Kailua and Lanikai.
